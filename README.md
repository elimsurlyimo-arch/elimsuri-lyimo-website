# Elly Lyimo Group Website

This repository contains the plan and assets for building a **high-converting website** for **Elly Lyimo Group**, a food company based in Tanzania. The website is designed to showcase products, attract customers, and increase conversions through optimized UX, branding, and content.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

- **Company Name:** Elly Lyimo Group  
- **Industry:** Food & Beverages (Tanzania)  
- **Goal:** Build a professional, user-friendly, and conversion-focused website.  
- **Target Audience:** Tanzanian consumers interested in quality food products.

---

## 📝 Project Prompt

The website plan was created based on the following prompt:

> Create a **full, structured plan** to build a **high-converting website** for **Elly Lyimo Group**, a food company based in Tanzania. The plan should include all aspects of website development including strategy, design, content, marketing, and conversion optimization. The website must reflect the company’s brand identity, appeal to Tanzanian consumers, and encourage sales and inquiries. Structure the plan as follows:
>
> 1. **Business & Audience Analysis** – Define target audience, competitors, and USPs.  
> 2. **Brand & Messaging Strategy** – Brand tone, colors, typography, key messaging.  
> 3. **Website Structure & UX** – Sitemap, navigation, wireframes, CTAs.  
> 4. **Content Strategy** – Text, images, SEO-friendly blog/recipe ideas.  
> 5. **Conversion Optimization** – Lead capture, trust-building, analytics.  
> 6. **Technical Recommendations** – CMS, mobile responsiveness, integrations.  
> 7. **Marketing & Growth Strategy** – Social media, email marketing, local SEO.  
> 8. **Visual Mockups & Branding** – Hero images, product photography, icons.  
> 9. **Launch & Post-Launch Plan** – Pre-launch checklist, post-launch growth, maintenance.

---

## ⚡ Features

- Mobile-first and responsive design  
- Clear calls-to-action to drive sales and inquiries  
- SEO-friendly structure for Tanzanian market  
- Integration with social media, payment gateways, and WhatsApp contact  
- Trust-building elements like testimonials, certifications, and reviews
